Rocket Analytics Tools — Performance Analysts

📌 Our Mission

We are a team of performance analysts dedicated to helping players unlock their full potential through advanced telemetry and real-time visual insights. Our tools are designed to provide clear, actionable data to improve gameplay and decision-making.

🛠️ What We Do

We develop lightweight, non-intrusive performance analyzers that integrate seamlessly with your gaming setup. Our focus is on delivering reliable, accurate metrics that help you understand and optimize your performance.

  • Real-time telemetry and visual overlays
  • Performance tracking and session analysis
  • Customizable HUD and data export
  • Regular updates for compatibility and stability

🔒 Privacy & Safety

We take privacy seriously. Our tools operate locally and do not collect or transmit personal data. All analytics are processed in real-time and are never stored or shared.
